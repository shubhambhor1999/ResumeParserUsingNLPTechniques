About Us


Hirex.com is the most innovative and second largest online job portal in India. Founded in 2010, over the past decade, Hirex.com has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 3.4 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site. Hirex.com connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, Hirex focuses on the entire career growth of candidates. To this end, Hirex has launched E-Learning- India’s largest career skills site for working professionals with over 500+ courses & certifications. As the industry shifts towards mobile, Hirex.com is leading the transition and is the fastest growing job portal on mobile devices, witnessing a 100% YOY growth in mobile traffic and also offers on-the-go jobs through the Shine Job Search App. Hirex works closely to bridge the gap between talent & opportunities and offers end-to-end recruitment solutions. Hirex Job Fair brings candidates and top employers under one roof. While Hirex HR Conclave brings top HR leaders to share insights on latest trends, innovations & best practices in the HR industry. Hirex also has a large reach through its print product, Hirex Jobs – the Tuesday Job supplement of Hindustan Times– making it the only job portal with an integrated print and online offering.
